Steps To Make A Basic Cake

First, make sure to have ALL of your Ingredients

You will need ingredients such as ...

  • 2 and 2/3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/3 cup of white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
    (or chocolate extract for a chocolate cake)
  • 3/4 cups of milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 pinches of salt
  • 1/2 cup of butter(unsalted)
  • Baking spray,or any substiute
    to coat your baking mold

Prepwork:

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees
and spray/coat your baking mold with
baking spray or any substitute

Step 1

Mix Dry Ingredients

Mix the all purpose flour, baking
powder and salt.

Step 2

Mix Wet Ingredients

In a seperate bowl mix your eggs,
butter, sugar and eggs

Step 3

Mix Your Milk!

In either another bowl or measuring cup
mix your milk and vanilla/chocolate
extract

Step 4

All together now

Once you liquids are well mixed
, a little at a time, add some
of your dry mixture and milk mixture
until they are all together in one
bowl.

Step 5

Make and Back Your Cake

The final step, pore your
batter/mixture into the baking mold
and put it into the oven.
Leave it in there for 30 to 40 minutes.
feel free to use a tooth pick
to check if the cake is ready.
you would do this by poking the cake,
if the inside is liquidy let the cake stay
and if its just right,
take it out and enjoy!
